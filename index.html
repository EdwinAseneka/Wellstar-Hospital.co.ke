About Wellstar Hospital

Located at Utawala Shopping Mall along Eastern Bypass, Wellstar Hospital has been serving Nairobi's Eastlands community since 2010. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare with compassion and innovation.

With 120 beds and 35 specialist consultants, we offer comprehensive medical services in all major specialties. Our hospital is accredited by the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council, ensuring the highest standards of patient care and safety.

Our Departments

Specialized care across various medical disciplines

Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive cardiac care with state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic facilities. Our team of cardiologists, cardiac surgeons, and support staff are dedicated to providing the highest quality care for patients with heart conditions.

  • Non-invasive cardiac diagnostics
  • Interventional cardiology
  • Cardiac rehabilitation
  • Electrophysiology studies
  • Preventive cardiology

We participate in international cardiac research studies to bring cutting-edge treatments to our patients.

Neurology Department

Our Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system. We offer advanced care for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and neuromuscular disorders.

  • Stroke care unit
  • EEG and EMG services
  • Botulinum toxin therapy
  • Neurological rehabilitation
  • Sleep disorder evaluation
